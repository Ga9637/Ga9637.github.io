Food For Thought

Good nutrition is a lifestyle not a diet. It starts with a new thinking about food

Do not approach your nutrition plan as a diet plan but rather a lifestyle change

Portion your meals and track your calories, there are many apps available to help with that

Control Impulsive urges to buy junk food and instead allow yourself one cheat meal a week

What to Eat

Use the following as a guideline

  • protein, such as eggs, chicken and lean red meat
  • Vegetables, but avoid corn and peas
  • Fruit
  • Fat, such as olive oil,nuts, and seeds

Limit the consumption of bread,pasta, white potatoes, and processed food

When you shop for groceries, shop the outside aisles and buy organic foods

Meal of the Week

Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast (organic preferred)
  • 1 tsp herbes de provence
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tst sea salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a plastic bag and marinate for at least half an hour.Preheat oven to 350, bake at least 30-35 min
